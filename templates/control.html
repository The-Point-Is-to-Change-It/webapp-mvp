Control the TV

TV Control Rules:

You have control of the TV if:
1. You turned it on AND you are watching it or have only been away for less than 10 minutes.
You do NOT have control of the TV if:
2. You turned it on BUT you have been away for more than 10 minutes.
3. You tuned it off.
You can gain control of the TV if:
1. No one currently has control and you turn it on (must turn it off and on if it's already on).
